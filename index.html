Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

After you log in using Facebook, we collect the following data:

  • Your public Facebook profile information (name, profile picture)
  • The list of Facebook Pages you manage (Page ID and name)
  • Page Insights data such as impressions, reach, followers (fans), engagement
  • Content-level data: Top 5 posts and their metrics (reach, link clicks, etc.)
  • Facebook Ads performance data, including:
    • Campaign ID, Campaign Name
    • Ad Set ID, Ad Set Name
    • Ad ID, Ad Name
    • Performance metrics like CTR, CPM, Impressions, Reach, and Spend

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data strictly to show a performance dashboard that helps users analyze their Facebook Page and Ads performance. The information is used to provide insights and visual reports only. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

We never publish or post on your behalf.

3. Data Sharing and Security

Your data is not shared with any third party and is stored securely using encrypted methods. Access is restricted to authenticated users only. We store data only as long as needed to provide analytics features.

4. Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time from your Facebook Settings.
