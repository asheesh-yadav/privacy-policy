Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2025

1. About Omnidash

Omnidash is a web-based analytics dashboard that integrates with multiple social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube to help users monitor their account performance, content engagement, and audience growth.

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you connect your social accounts.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Facebook & Instagram

  • Public profile information (name, profile picture)
  • List of managed Pages and Instagram Business accounts
  • Page & profile insights (reach, impressions, engagement, followers)
  • Post-level metrics (likes, comments, shares, clicks)
  • Ads performance data (if connected), including:
    • Campaign name and ID
    • Ad set name and ID
    • Ad name and ID
    • Metrics such as CTR, CPM, reach, spend

2.2 YouTube

After you connect your YouTube account, we collect:

  • Channel information (name, profile picture, description)
  • Subscriber count
  • Total views and video count
  • Daily analytics:
    • Views
    • Watch time
    • Average view duration
    • Subscribers gained / lost
    • Likes and comments
  • Video-level analytics:
    • Views
    • Likes & comments
    • Engagement rate
    • Retention rate
    • Watch time

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to:

  • Display analytics dashboards
  • Generate performance insights
  • Show growth trends and reports

We do NOT:

  • Post content on your behalf
  • Modify your social accounts
  • Sell or share your data

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard security practices. Access is restricted to authenticated users only.

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide analytics features.

5. User Control

You can revoke access anytime:

  • Facebook: Facebook App Settings
  • Google / YouTube: Google App Permissions

6. Third-Party APIs

Omnidash uses official APIs provided by:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram)
  • Google (YouTube Data API & YouTube Analytics API)

All data access follows their platform policies.
